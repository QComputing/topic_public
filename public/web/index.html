Quantum Computing: Implementation

Quantum computing has been a hot topic for a long time. They work by taking advantage of quantum physics in order to search through all the potential possibilities in a given problem. It is probably one of the most desired technology for our century with Artificial Intelligence. It is so powerful that it would break the security ciphers we use today, however it would open up door to a new era of discoveries.

The Implementation of a Quantum Computer

This website aims to giving insights on the different layers of abstractions that would make a Quantum Computer functional. From it's hardware to its software, let's discover the true potential of these machines and the prospects they would bring to our world.
It's a race, for companies and research groups all around the world to demonstrate a reliable Quantum Computer. IBM has been making headlines recently about a free to access Quantum Processor from the cloud... Is the future in the past of the present now?
